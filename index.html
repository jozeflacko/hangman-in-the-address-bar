"Hangman in the Address Bar" is a address bar guessing game for one player against the address bar. Your address bar thinks of a word, phrase or sentence and you should try to guess it by suggesting letters, within a certain number of guesses.
To play a new game simply click on the button "New Game" In the address bar you can see:

  • hangman figure
  • hangman word - this work you should find
  • left attempts - how many incorrect letters you can still choose before hangman will hang :(
